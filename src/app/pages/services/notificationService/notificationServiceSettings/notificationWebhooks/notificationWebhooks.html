Notifications are triggered by Rehive webhooks. In order to correctly trigger a notification you should ensure you have at least one webhook of the same event type as the notification event. All notification webhooks should be configured with the company secret because it ensures secure communication between Rehive and the service.

Note: The "secret" field will automatically get filled with the company secret by using the below webhook shortcut buttons or you can manually copy the company secret and paste it in the webhook forms ensuring secure communication between Rehive Platform and the service.
